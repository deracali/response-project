Business Borrowing

Business Line of Credit

Perfect for short-term needs like cash flow, inventory, and payroll, this revolving line of credit allows you to borrow, repay, and borrow again without having to reapply. Interest-only payments available.1

To apply, please visit a branch near you or send an email to smallbusinesslending@afbank.com.

Business Term Loan

A term loan can help your business consolidate debt or purchase capital and equipment, making it ideal for longer term financial needs. Payments for this loan are based on a predetermined schedule with monthly principal and interest payments.1

To apply, please visit a branch near you or send an email to smallbusinesslending@afbank.com.

SBA Loans

Armed Forces Bank is proud to offer SBA loans which are guaranteed by the Small Business Administration. To learn more about SBA 7(a) loans and SBA 504 loans, click here.

To get started, send an email to SBALoansAFB@afbank.com.

Business Credit Card

Make and keep track of everyday purchases in one place, reduce reconciliation time, and avoid having to manage numerous invoices and payments to vendors with a business credit card.1​

1 All loans and lines of credit subject to credit approval and require automatic payment deduction from an Armed Forces Bank business checking account. Origination and annual fees may apply.
