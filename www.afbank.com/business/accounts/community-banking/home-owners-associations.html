Community Banking

Home Owners Associations

We’re a neighbor, not just a bank. Our dedicated Home Owners Association team focuses on forming partnerships with HOA boards and residents like yours. See below for our full suite of products and services, which can be tailored to the needs of your HOA.

Features

HOA Checking Accounts - Low cost checking services

HOA Premier Money Market - Maximize your interest potential with our high-earning money market. See current rates and requirements.

Double FDIC Coverage - Our two bank charters mean double the FDIC coverage.

Collateralized Deposits - Feature deposit insurance beyond FDIC limits

HOA Loans - For renovations, repairs, or other cash flow needs

ACH Origination - Speed up your collection process by allowing residents to pay dues electronically.

Bill Pay - Efficiently pay vendors through our online bill pay service. See additional treasury management services.

HOA Lockbox - Let us take away the stress of handling all the checks that come in for dues.
