Personal Loans

Express Loan

Designed specifically for those who may have low credit, an Express Loan can provide access to much-needed cash without breaking your budget or resorting to high-interest payday lenders. You can use the funds to help with tuition, unexpected medical or car expenses, or anything else you need.

Learn More

Quick access to cash with our automated online application

Features

Loan amount of $1,250

Low credit scores considered with a satisfactory account relationship with us and direct deposit

A great alternative to high-interest, short-term lenders like payday lending offices

Automated payments so you'll never miss one

Additional Features

  • Fixed terms and monthly payments to simplify your budget

  • Automatic reporting to the three major credit bureaus to help you build a credit history

What You Need to Know

There is a $35 non-refundable application fee.

A direct deposit is required.

We recommend applying for this loan once your direct deposit has been established. That way, we can consider your account relationship with us in lieu of good credit.

When applying, if you’re an active duty military member without military housing:

  • For your income, from your LES, list your monthly "Total Entitlements.”
  • For your residential status, list the appropriate type and monthly expense.

When applying, if you’re an active duty military member with military housing and an allotment for privatized housing:

  • For your income, from your LES, list your income as your "Total Entitlements" minus your “BAH."
  • For your residential status, list "Other" and enter the amount as "$0.00."
