Home Equity Loans

Home Equity Line of Credit

Get 2.49% APR introductory rate for 6 months
7.49% APR variable rate after introductory period1

 

An Armed Forces Bank Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC) allows for quick access to funds from your home’s equity. Paying for home improvements, debt consolidations or education expenses is easy with this flexible line of credit. Borrow up to 85% of your home’s value, and pay lower interest rates than most credit cards. Enjoy additional benefits such as low to no closing costs, and a 6-month introductory rate.  


See "What You Should Know About Home Equity Lines of Credit" (PDF).

Not available in TX.
