Savings Account

Armed Forces Bank offers a simple Savings Account with returns you can count on. Plus, additional features can help your manage your finances and build your rainy day fund or nest egg. Digital Banking allows you to set up and customize Account Alerts2 so you are always in the know about your daily balance, transactions and any unusual account activity. Open your Savings Account today and bank on the go.

Take advantage of our Saving Cents program that allows you to round up your debit card purchases from your Armed Forces Bank Checking Account and apply the change directly into your Savings Account.

Applying typically takes 10 minutes or less.

What You Need to Know

  • A $25 minimum opening deposit is required.
  • No monthly service charge when you maintain a minimum balance of $100 per cycle. Otherwise, a $5 monthly service charge applies.
  • Free e.Statements or $5 paper statements.
  • Enroll in Saving Cents to waive the minimum opening deposit and $5 monthly service charge for 12 months.
  • Closing new accounts within 90 days of opening will result in a $20 early closure fee.
