Student Savings

Establishing healthy financial habits from a young age can set up children and young adults for future financial success.  Get a head start on savings and financial education with a Student Savings Account with Armed Forces Bank for children and young adults under the age of 23.  Joint ownership with a parent or guardian is required for those under the age of 17.  Interest compounds quarterly1 and there is no monthly service charge. A minimum opening deposit of $25 is required.  

Take advantage of our Saving Cents program that rounds up debit card purchases from an Armed Forces Bank Checking Account and applies the change directly into a Savings Account. Students can see their money grow over time by signing up for Digital Banking and setting up customized account alerts3.
