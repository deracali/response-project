Safe Spending Account

The Safe Spending Account from Armed Forces Bank offers you the convenience and flexibility you need for daily personal banking without Overdraft fees.  Plus, you'll have free access to online banking, mobile deposit1, bill pay and account alerts3. 

Armed Forces Bank clients can also bank at over 70 Academy Bank locations and deposit cast at over 4,000 Walmart locations at the register.  In addition you'll have free access to 30,000+ ATMs though our MoneyPassNetwork2.

Open Now

Applying typically takes 10 minutes or less.

Features

Free mobile check deposit1

No Overdraft Fees
Also No Account Activation, Closure
Dormacy, or Low Balance Fees

No fee money orders for existing clients

Free, unlimited access to 30,000+ MoneyPass® Network2 ATMs — find one with our mobile app or the MoneyPass® site

Cash2DebitTM - add cash to your account from any Walmart location at the cashier4

Additional Features

  • Military Early Pay

  • Free e.Statements or $2 paper statements

  • Online Banking with Bill Pay

  • Free Visa® debit card with EMV chip technology

  • Locations around the country, including branches inside Exchanges that are open weekends and evenings

What You Need to Know

  • There is a $25 opening deposit requirement.
  • There is a $5 monthly recurring service charge.
  • Paper checks are not included with this account.  You have an option to purchase paper checks at any time.
  • If you choose to receive paper statements a $2.00 paper statement fee will apply.
  • When using Cash2Debit™ to make a cash deposit using your debit card at a Wal-Mart cashier, a $4.95 per transaction fee may apply. 
  • In-network ATMs are free and out-of-network ATM fees are only $2.50.

Open Now

Applying typically takes 10 minutes or less.

2 No fees for withdrawals, transfers, balance inquiries or denied ATM transactions at ATMs in the MoneyPass® Network if you have a MoneyPass® logo on the back of your card. MoneyPass® Network ATMs are within the U.S. only.

3 Message and data rates charged by your mobile phone carrier may apply.

4 A $4.95 fee may apply. Deposit limits and restrictions apply. Not available in Vermont. Ask an Academy Bank associate for details. Not available at self-checkout.
