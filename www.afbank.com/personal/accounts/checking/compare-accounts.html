Saving Cents

Round up your debit card purchases automatically and put the change in your savings with Saving Cents.

How It Works

  • Choose - Choose how much you'd like to round up your debit card purchases from your checking account. Options range from the nearest $1 to $5 increment.
  • Save - Every time you make a purchase, we’ll round it up. Each night all the extra change will automatically transfer from your checking to your savings.
  • Track - See what you saved on your bank statement each month.

To sign up, call or visit a branch, or log into Digital Banking and send a Secure Message.

Overdraft Protection

An overdraft occurs when there’s not enough money in an account to cover a transaction. We can cover your overdrafts in two different ways:

  • We have standard overdraft practices that apply to all of our accounts.
  • We also offer overdraft protection plans, such as a link to a savings account, or to an overdraft line of credit, which may be less expensive than our standard overdraft practices.

To learn more, ask us about these plans or click on the Overdraft Resources links.
